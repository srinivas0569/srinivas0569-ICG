Who Are We ?

ICG Design

is a division of Iconic Creative Group LLC

ICG Design is a design and development agency that specializes in user friendly, professional, responsive and smart websites and mobile applications for their customers to easily navigate. Founded in 2013, ICG designs has worked with small, mid-sized and fortune 500 business in their growth by providing digital solution to promote their business. We also work with individuals to provide lowcost high-end designs for their web and mobile applications.
